# Retail Mart KPI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes retail business performance across sales, profitability, customer growth, and operational metrics.

The dashboard provides insights into:

- Revenue
- COGS
- Gross Profit
- Net Profit
- Operating Expense
- Marketing Spend
- Customer Acquisition
- Repeat Customers
- Returns
- Inventory Value
- Online vs Offline Revenue

---

## Key Insights

• Revenue increased from 1,718M to 1,973M in 2024 (+14.86%)
• Net Profit increased by 22.78%
• Electronics generated highest revenue
• Returns increased by 7.38%
• Online revenue contributed over 50% of total sales
